CSCE 567 - Final Project - Retail Sales Analysis

by Madeline Tharp

These visualizations examine fictional retail sales data, focusing on the full year from March 2023 to February 2024. Some key takeaways were:

  • Sales by State - Connecticut, Maine, Georgia, Kansas, and New Mexico had the highest sales by state

  • Sales by Product Category - Forecasted sales appear to be higher than the expected downward trend

  • Sales by Customer Segment - Most sales are coming from regular customers, and there is room to improve their ratings

  • Transactions by Shipping Method - Similar distributions across all shipping methods and ratings

  • Transactions by Payment Method - Credit cards lead in number of transactions, followed by debit card, cash, and paypal

  • Transactions by Hour - Standard across each hour, shows limitations of fictional data
